Want to learn to code, but don't know where to begin? Check out codecademy.com, a free online resource for learning the basics of most popular languages and frameworks. Languages included are HTML & CSS, Javacript, Ruby, Python, and PHP, among others.

Have a weird bug or need to do something very specific for a browser but don't know where to start? browserhacks.com is a great place to look that's full of info and tricks to help you fix your bugs and get your website cross-browser compatible.
